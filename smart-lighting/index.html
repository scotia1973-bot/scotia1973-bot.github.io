📚 Smart Lighting Buying Guide

How to Choose Smart Lighting

Choosing the right smart lighting depends on your ecosystem, budget, and room layout. Here's what to consider:

  • 🔗 Ecosystem: Apple HomeKit, Alexa, or Google Home?
  • 📡 Connectivity: Wi-Fi bulbs (no hub) vs Zigbee/Thread (hub needed)
  • 💡 Bulb Type: RGB colour, tunable white, or warm white only
  • 📏 Brightness: Lumens matter — aim for 800+ for main lighting
  • 🔋 Power: Hardwired switches vs battery sensors vs plug-in
  • 💰 Budget: Start with 1–2 bulbs, expand with strips and sensors
Top Smart Lighting Tips
  • ✨ Use motion sensors in hallways and bathrooms for hands-free lighting
  • 🛏️ Set routines — gradual wake-up light in morning, warm dim at night
  • 🎮 Add TV backlighting for immersive gaming and movie nights
  • 🏠 Combine strips + bulbs for layered ambient and task lighting
  • 🔐 Vacation mode — simulate occupancy with random light schedules
