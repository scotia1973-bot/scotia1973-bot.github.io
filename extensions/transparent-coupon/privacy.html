Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

🔒 Minimal Data Collection

Transparent Coupon collects only the bare minimum needed to show you deals. We store just the store domain you're visiting (e.g., "amazon.com") so we can fetch available coupons. No browsing history, no personal information, no tracking.

What Data We Collect

We collect the absolute minimum required to function:

🛍️

Store Domain (Collected)

When you visit a supported e-commerce site, we detect the store domain (e.g., "amazon.com") to fetch relevant coupon codes and price history. This is sent to our API server. We do not track which specific pages you visit within a store.

🚫

Never Collected

No browsing history, no personal information (name, email, address, payment info), no browsing habits, no search queries, no login credentials, no cookies, no analytics, no tracking pixels.

What Data Is Stored Locally

The following data is stored on your device using chrome.storage.local and never transmitted anywhere:

  • Theme preference — dark or light mode
  • Price alerts — which products you want to be notified about
  • Savings & commission stats — your personal running totals
  • Settings — auto-apply toggle, badge visibility, notification preferences

Data Sent to Our Servers

When you visit a supported store, the extension sends a minimal request to our coupon API:

  • Store domain — e.g., "amazon.com" (to fetch available coupons)
  • Anonymous usage statistics — aggregated, non-identifiable data about which coupons are most popular (used to improve our service)

We do not log IP addresses. We do not use cookies or any tracking mechanisms. We do not build profiles of your shopping behavior.

Affiliate Link Disclosure

When you click a deal link through Transparent Coupon, we may add our own affiliate tag to the URL. The store then knows you came from Transparent Coupon and may pay us a commission if you make a purchase.

This does not increase the price you pay. It also means the store learns about your purchase only if you click an affiliate link — we do not transmit any information about your browsing to affiliate networks otherwise.

We never modify or hijack existing affiliate links on any page. Your content creators keep their commissions.

Third-Party Services

The extension communicates with the following services:

  • api.gadgethumans.com — Our own API server for coupon codes, price history, and store detection. No personal data transmitted.
  • api.coupon-partner.com — A third-party coupon aggregation partner. Only the store domain is sent; no personal data is shared. This service is subject to its own privacy policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties. The only data shared is the store domain sent to our API partners solely for the purpose of fetching available coupon codes.

Data Retention

Any store domain data sent to our servers is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored in any identifiable form. Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics may be retained for service improvement.

Local storage data (settings, alerts, savings stats) remains on your device until you clear it or uninstall the extension.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through the extension's update notes.
