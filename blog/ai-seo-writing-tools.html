Best AI SEO Writing Tools in 2026

SEO content writing has been transformed by AI. Modern tools don't just write — they research keywords, analyze competitors, optimize for featured snippets, and target AI search engines. We tested the leading platforms.

Surfer SEO + AI: Best for Data-Driven Content

Surfer's AI Writer combines its powerful content analysis engine with AI generation. You enter a keyword, Surfer analyzes the top 20 results, and generates content optimized to outperform them. The real-time content score shows exactly how well your content matches ranking signals.

Frase: Best for Research & Briefs

Frase excels at the research phase — it generates comprehensive content briefs with the questions, keywords, and subtopics that top-ranking pages cover. The AI Writer then generates content that follows the brief. The integration with Google Search Console shows which content needs updating.
