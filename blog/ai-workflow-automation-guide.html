AI Workflow Automation Guide: Zapier vs n8n vs Make 2026

Automate Your Repetitive Tasks with AI

Workflow automation platforms have added powerful AI capabilities, making it possible to automate tasks that previously required manual intervention. We tested Zapier, n8n, and Make to find the best AI automation platform.

Zapier Central: Best for Non-Developers

Zapier's AI features let you describe what you want in plain English and it builds the automation. "When I get an email with an invoice attachment, save it to Google Drive and create a Slack notification" — Zapier handles the mapping automatically. At $29.99/month for Starter, it's accessible.

n8n: Best for Developers

n8n is open-source and self-hostable, giving you complete control. Its AI node lets you integrate with any LLM API for custom automation logic. The AI Agent node can make decisions based on your data — route leads, classify emails, or generate responses. Free for self-hosted, $20/month for cloud.

Make (formerly Integromat): Best Visual Builder

Make's visual builder with AI integration lets you design complex multi-branch workflows. The AI tools module supports OpenAI, Claude, and Hugging Face for text generation, classification, and extraction within your workflows.
