Last updated: May 7, 2026

What Information We Collect

We collect no personal information actively. GadgetHumans does not have user accounts, does not require registration, and does not use tracking cookies or analytics scripts.

The only technical data we may access comes from Google Search Console, which is used solely for site verification and to understand general search performance trends (e.g., which pages appear in search results). This data is aggregated and anonymized — we do not track individual visitors.

  • No user accounts — We don't ask for or store your name, email address, or any personal details.
  • No cookies — We do not set first-party or third-party tracking cookies (excluding Google Search Console verification, which operates at the domain level, not the visitor level).
  • No analytics scripts — We do not run Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any similar tracking service.

How We Use Information

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to use, share, or sell. Any anonymized, aggregated data from Google Search Console is used only to improve our content and understand which topics our audience finds helpful.
