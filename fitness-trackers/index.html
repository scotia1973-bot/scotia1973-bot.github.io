📚 Fitness Tracker Buying Guide

How to Choose a Fitness Tracker

The best fitness tracker depends on your goals, preferred activities, and budget. Here's what to consider:

  • 🎯 Goals: Weight loss, training, sleep improvement, or general wellness?
  • 📡 GPS: Built-in GPS is essential for runners and cyclists
  • ❤️ Heart rate: Optical HR sensors vary in accuracy — check reviews
  • 🔋 Battery life: 5–7 days is standard; GPS watches last 1–2 weeks
  • 📱 App ecosystem: Fitbit, Garmin Connect, Apple Health, or Samsung Health?
  • 💧 Water resistance: 5ATM for swimming, IP68 for showers/splashes
Top Fitness Tracker Tips
  • 🌙 Wear your tracker to bed for sleep tracking — it's the most underrated feature
  • 📉 Focus on trends, not daily numbers — week-over-week data is more meaningful
  • ⚡ Enable continuous HR monitoring for accurate calorie burn estimates
  • 📲 Sync with MyFitnessPal or Strava for deeper analytics
  • 🔄 Update firmware regularly — manufacturers add features post-launch
