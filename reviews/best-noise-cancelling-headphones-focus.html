Our Pick: Sony WH-1000XM5

The Sony XM5 wins for most remote workers because it simply has no significant weakness. Best-in-class ANC, all-day comfort at 250g, 41-hour battery that lasts a full work week, excellent call quality, and multipoint Bluetooth for laptop + phone.

When to Choose Bose QC Ultra

If you have a larger head or wear glasses, the Bose QC Ultra's slightly deeper ear cups and lighter clamping force may be more comfortable for marathon sessions.

When to Choose AirPods Max

Only if you're deeply invested in Apple's ecosystem (iPhone + Mac + Apple TV) and the seamless switching is worth the $549 price premium.
