Your webcam is your handshake in the remote work world. A blurry, poorly-lit video feed undermines your professionalism regardless of how good your ideas are. Upgrading from a built-in laptop camera is the single highest-ROI improvement you can make to your remote setup.

Logitech Brio 4K: Best Overall

The Brio 4K delivers 4K resolution at 30fps or 1080p at 60fps, with excellent color accuracy and auto-focus. RightLight 4 technology handles challenging lighting conditions well. The 90° field of view is ideal for showing you + your workspace.

Elgato Facecam: Best for Low Light

The Facecam uses a large Sony STARVIS sensor that excels in low light. It offers 1080p at 60fps with no auto-focus hunting (fixed focus — set once and forget). The Camera Hub software gives you full manual control over exposure, white balance, and ISO.

Quick Tips for Better Video Calls

  • Position your webcam at eye level (stack it on books if needed)
  • Face a window for natural key lighting
  • Avoid backlight (don't sit with a window behind you)
  • A dedicated microphone matters more than camera quality for perceived professionalism
