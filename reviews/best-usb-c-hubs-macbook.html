Why You Need a USB-C Hub for Your MacBook M4

Frequently Asked Questions

Does a USB-C hub work with MacBook Pro M4?

Yes. All Thunderbolt 4 and USB-C hubs are fully compatible with the MacBook Pro M4 and MacBook Air M4. The M4 chip supports DisplayPort Alt Mode over USB-C, which powers the video output on these hubs.

Can I charge my MacBook through a USB-C hub?

Most hubs support Power Delivery (PD) passthrough, which means you plug your MacBook charger into the hub, and the hub charges the laptop while providing ports. Look for hubs with 85W+ PD passthrough for the MacBook Pro 14" and 100W+ for the 16".

Does a hub affect MacBook performance?

No. USB-C and Thunderbolt hubs don't affect CPU or GPU performance. However, some cheap hubs can cause display flickering or disconnect issues — stick to brands we've tested above (UGREEN, CalDigit, Anker, Satechi).
